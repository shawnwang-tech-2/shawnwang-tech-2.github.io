BeHive X ACYA 北京 "Perfect your Elevator Pitch" Workshop, Nov 29

2017-11-23 activities

Not sure how to get your ideas across and have others share your enthusiasm for a business venture or university assignment? Or are you worried about making your work and study experience “relevant” in an upcoming job interview? Don’t let fears of being tongue-tied drag you down!
ACYA Beijing and BeHive have teamed up to bring you an exciting workshop on “Perfecting your Elevator Pitch”- giving you the chance to learn actionable skills to improve your pitch for interviews, client meetings and with direct customer interactions. Led by a seasoned start-up mentor, TEDxBeijing speaker, and CEO speechwriter Martin Barnes, the session will centre around an hour-long workshop from Martin, covering:
Behive x ACYA 北京：如何完善自己的电梯间演讲
不知道如何在企业风险或大学作业中和别人分享你的想法与热情？担心在即将到来的工作面试中，你的工作和学习经验没有“相关之处”？不要让舌头打结把你拖下水！
ACYA 北京与BeHive为您举办一次激动人心的“如何完善你的电梯间演讲”讲习班 - 给你一次学习可操作性的技巧的机会，提高面试、客户会议和直接客户互动的能力。由经验丰富的 TEDx北京演讲嘉宾，首席执行官演讲稿撰写人，创业导师Martin Barnes领导，我们将举行一小时的讲习班，内容包括：
Why - we pitch

Who - we pitch to and what they need

Where - we can be effective

How - to structure a pitch

When - to pitch and how long

What - we need to achieve from the pitch

哪里 – 我们可以最有效应？

如何 – 形制完美的电梯间演讲？

什么时候 - 电梯间演讲，要多久？

Feeling a little daunted? Worry not! This isn’t just a theory-filled lecture on the art of the pitch! At the end, volunteers will have a chance to try out what they learned in a simulated pitch challenge (with prizes, of course!) and instant feedback and tips from Martin! Spaces are limited so make sure to RSVP by Nov 28 by scanning the QR Code below. We look forward to seeing you on Wednesday!

About Martin & Mol

Martin Barnes, a London-trained graphic designer and TEDx speaker, has designed a host of brand identities and now focuses on helping you successfully brand your ideas through your presentation and pitches. He is the founder of MoI, Mountains of Imagination (MoI) which specializes in visualization and making sure you enjoy your future spotlight moments! They believe that if you enjoy your spotlight moment your audience will enjoy it as well.

Martin, TEDx的演说家，同时来自受训于伦敦的美术设计师，已经设计了许多的品牌形象。目前关注于通过个人的展示和想法，为个人打响品牌。
MoI的创始人, 精通图像化，如图表设计, 幻灯片展示、广告设计等，以确保你喜欢未来自己展示自己的时刻！如果你喜欢自己表现展示自己的时候，你的观众也会同样地喜欢！

The Australia China Youth Association (ACYA) is a NFP run for members, by members, that works to actively foster a transnational community of young Australians and Chinese interested in further understanding each other’s countries. Focusing chiefly on secondary school students, university students, and early career professionals, ACYA’s objectives are to facilitate and develop lasting bilateral friendships, partnerships and opportunities across a variety of social sectors, including but not limited to: business, academia, government, sport and the arts.
